## How to Use This Document

This profile captures taste — it's not a checklist.

### Frequency guidance:

- **Hard rule** — Never violate these (your Hard Nos and Dead Phrases)
- **Strong tendency** (use 70-80% of the time) — Your structural preferences, emotional target, humor style
- **Light preference** — Specific word choices, punctuation details

### Context matters:

Your voice adapts to format. Note how it shifts between:
- Social media posts
- Emails
- Reports / long-form
- Different languages

### The litmus test:

> "Does this sound like something I would actually write — or does it sound like an AI trying hard to imitate me?"

If it feels forced, pull back. Less imitation, more inhabitation.
